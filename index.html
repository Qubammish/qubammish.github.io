WHO AM I?

My parents named me Ammishaddai Ofori, 8 days after I was born according to Akan tradition.
I am the second born and the first male child in a family of seven.
I am passionate about technology and human interactions. I love #spokenWordPoetry, #twitter, #manga, #writing and lots of other geeky stuff.

I am currently a proud EIT (Entrepreneur in Training) at the Meltwater Entrepreneurial School of Technology (MEST)
What this means is that I write lines of code to change lives.
I am currently mastering skills in software development, business models and sales.

So go ahead! Browse through my online diary and have a fair idea of who I want you to think I am.
